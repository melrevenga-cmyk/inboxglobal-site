// Scope

What we review.

01

Identity and Authentication

SPF, DKIM, DMARC presence + alignment checks. Policy posture (none/quarantine/reject) and enforcement readiness. Quick flags for misalignment, weak identity, and governance gaps.

02

Domain + Mailbox Hygiene

Domain purpose separation (outbound vs brand vs transactional). Provider distribution and basic mailbox configuration risks. Overlap exposure points — reused identity, shared assets, unsafe routing.

03

Sending Behavior and Volume Risk

Daily volume posture, ramp logic, and pacing risk. Reply/complaint dilution patterns. Bounce and unknown-user risk indicators. Basic suppression expectations.

04

List and Targeting Risk Signals

High-level list source risk assessment (cold vs warm vs internal). Basic hygiene expectations and obvious leak points — stale lists, role accounts, weak suppression, poor segmentation.

05

Monitoring Coverage

What you're not tracking today and should be. Where performance will degrade before you notice. Minimum viable monitoring and enforcement checkpoints.
